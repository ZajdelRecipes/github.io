Stuffed Chicken Breast with Mushroom Sauce

Ingredients
1 2/3 cups of hot water
4 tablespoons of margarine cut into pieces
1 pacckage of stove top stuffing
1 beaten egg
6 thin-sliced skinless boneless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons of melted margarine
1 can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup of water
1/4 teaspoon of dill weed

Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

2. Stir hot water, 4 tablespoons of margarine and contents of stuffing seasoning packet into bowl until margarine is melted.

3. Add stuffing crumbs and eggs and stir until moistened.

4. Spoon stuffing onto each breast, wrap chicken around stuffing and place seam-side down in a greased shallow pan.

5. Brush with melted butter and place any remaining stuffing in center of dish.

6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes until chicken is thoroughly cooked.

7. Heat soup, water and dill weed in saucepan.

8. Serve over chicken.
