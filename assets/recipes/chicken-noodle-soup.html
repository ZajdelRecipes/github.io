Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

2 chicken breasts with bone and skin
1 package of soup noodles
1 stalk of celery
1 package of baby carrots
2 onions
1 package of chicken noodle soup secrets
2 16 oz cans of chicken broth

Directions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.

2. Add the 2 chicken breasts to the pot of water and boil until the chicken is thoroughly cooked.

3. Peel the 2 onions and add to the boiling water.

4. Cut the celery and baby carrots into small chunks and add to the boiling water.

5. When the chicken is done remove from the pot and remove the chicken from the bone and add back to the pot.

6. Add the 2 cans of broth to the pot and let simmer.

7. Bring another pot of water to a boil and add the soup noodles.

8. Let the noodles boil for about 3 minutes stirring occasionally.

9. When the noodles are done add them to the pot of soup and serve.
