Stuffed Peppers

Ingredients

8 green peppers
1 lb fresh spinach
1 lb ground meat
1/2 minced onion
1/2 cup of Fine Dry Bread Crumbs
1/4 Cup of grated romano cheese
1 egg
1/4 teaspoon of salt and pepper
1 large can of tomato sauce

Directions

1. Wash peppers and remove the insides.

2. Steam peppers in a pot of water with lid on for 5 minutes.

4. Put spinach in a pot of water with lid on and boil for 5 minutes.

5. Drain the spinach and chop it finely.

6. Heat oil in a skillet.

7. Cook until the meat is done.

8. Put in a bowl.

9. Add spinach, bread crumbs, romano cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.

10. Mix well.

11. Fill each green pepper.

12. Grease 2 1/2 quart casserole.

13. Sprinkle with salt.

14. Pour tomato sauce over the green peppers.

15. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes.
