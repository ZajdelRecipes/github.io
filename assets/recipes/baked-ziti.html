Baked Ziti

Ingredients

1 16 oz, package of ziti
1 lb of ground beef
1 15 oz. container of ricotta cheese
1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese
1/4 cup of chopped parsley
1 egg slightly beaten
3/4 teaspoon of salt
1/4 teaspoon of pepper
2 15 oz. jars of spaghetti sauce
1 8 oz. package of shredded mozarella cheese

Directions

1. About 1 hour before serving in an 8 quart dutch oven, prepare ziti as labeled on directions, drain and set aside.

2. In same dutch oven over medium-high heat, cook meat until well-browned.

3. Remove dutch oven from heat.

4. Stir in ricotta and next 5 ingredients and half of spaghetti sauce until well-mixed.

5. Add ziti and toss gently to coat well.

6. Spoon mixture into a 13x9 inch pan and pour remaining sauce evenly over ziti mixture.

7. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.

8. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes until bubbly.
